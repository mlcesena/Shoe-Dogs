Our Background

Shoe Dogs is a sneaker resale website that allows customers to purchase the latest sneaker and apparel releases. Shoe Dogs aims to remove the common issues with buying resold shoes and apparel today. In addition to serving as a hosting platform for other users to sell their stock of shoes or clothing items, Shoe Dogs also maintains their own inventory of product. Products are then priced based on market demand and availability. While prices can fluctuate over time, users are provided a better experience as they do not have to always follow the bid format that other sneaker reselling sites follow.
